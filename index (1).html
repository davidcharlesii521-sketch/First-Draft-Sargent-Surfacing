What We Do

Our Services

🏭 01
Industrial Sandblasting

High-powered abrasive blasting for large-scale industrial projects. We handle heavy equipment, structural steel, and manufacturing components with precision and efficiency.

🔧 02
Rust Removal

Eliminate corrosion from metal surfaces quickly and completely. Our process removes rust down to bare metal, extending the life of your equipment and structures.

🎨 03
Paint Stripping

Complete removal of old paint, coatings, and finishes. Ideal prep for repainting, powder coating, or exposing original metal surfaces.

⚒️ 04
Surface Preparation

Proper surface prep is the foundation of any long-lasting finish. We ensure your surface is perfectly clean, profiled, and ready for coating or treatment.

🚜 05
Equipment Restoration

Restore farm equipment, trailers, vehicles, and machinery to like-new condition. We take pride in reviving what others write off.

✨ 06
Metal Cleaning

Deep cleaning of metal surfaces to remove grease, scale, weld spatter, and contaminants — leaving a clean, uniform surface ready for its next life.
